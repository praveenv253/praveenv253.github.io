Hi! I'm Praveen, a PhD. candidate in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department of Carnegie Mellon University. I work in Prof. Pulkit Grover's lab.

What do I do?

Broadly speaking, I'm interested in working at the intersection of signal processing, information theory, neuroscience and neuroengineering. For the last few years, I've been focused on problems with clinical applications. My advisor and I have worked closely with doctors, statisticians and neuroscientists to find better ways to diagnose brain diseases such as epilepsy and cortical spreading depolarizations.

To be a bit more technical, I've recently been working on understanding the fundamental limits of brain imaging systems, specifically EEG, and on designing algorithms for optimal brain signal reconstruction using EEG data. Earlier, I worked briefly on metrics for measuring information flow in the brain. Please visit my research page for more!
