--- layout: blog title: Log Message date: 2015-03-03 20:57:30 -0500 day: Tuesday tags: - log message - nautilus - hidden files - default --- Nautilus shows hidden files by default when opened from command line. To fix this, go to dconf-editor, and under org->gtk->settings->file-chooser, uncheck show-hidden.
