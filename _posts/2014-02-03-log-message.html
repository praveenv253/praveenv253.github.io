--- layout: blog title: Log Message date: 2014-02-03 19:00:46 +0530 day: Monday tags: - log message - gcc - preprocessor - macros --- You can save and restore macros by using #pragma push_macro("") and #pragma pop_macro("") respectively! This means that you can temporarily redefine macros! For example, I used this to temporarily redefine GSL_REAL to std::real, and then define it back once the need had passed.
