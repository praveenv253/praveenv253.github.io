National Service Scheme website

I worked on desiging the "backend" for the website used by the National Service Scheme chapter at IIT Madras. The website resembled an ERP system, with a requirement for heirarchy, differing permissions at different levels, mechanisms for awarding credits to volunteers, etc.

Shaastra 2011 website

During the summer of 2011, I worked on the backend of the Shaastra 2011 website. This website served as an interface between the organisers and participants of the technical fest, Shaastra 2011. Participants had to be able to log in, register for events and create teams, while organisers had to be able to review details of potential participants and perform a preliminary selection procedure involving an online test.
